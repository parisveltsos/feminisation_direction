The combined virgin and courted, head or abdomen DE genes between the EM sexual selection treatments, for each sex (4 sets), are generated by the last 4 lines in the `~/git/feminisation_direction/scripts/batch_constrain.sh` script

ie 

	Rscript ~/git/feminisation_direction/scripts/constrain.r E M M All B 0.1
	Rscript ~/git/feminisation_direction/scripts/constrain.r E M M All H 0.1
	Rscript ~/git/feminisation_direction/scripts/constrain.r E M F All B 0.1
	Rscript ~/git/feminisation_direction/scripts/constrain.r E M F All H 0.1
